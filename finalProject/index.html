Purpose & Goals

The first purpose of the Idaho Falls Chamber of Commerce is to advocate for businesses in the local community to help business grow and have our economy prosper. Second is to help educate and develop local leaders and businesses through seminars and workshops. The third purpose is to provide opportunities for business people to interact with one another and network. The last purpose is to help market Idaho Falls to tourists and people thinking about moving to this area. This chamber was founded in 1904 and has grown to over 600 member businesses and over 27,000 member business employees! The numbers show that we are doing all we can to help our local economy. Our current goal is continue this growth and help keep our local economy one that provides jobs and success for our residents!
